SP BUZZ

SP BUZZ offers you a selection of the latest news from one of the nation's leading tertiary insitutions. You get to find out the latest happenings on campus too via this Singapore Polytechnic (SP) mobile application.

SP BUZZ is a Singapore Polytechnic (SP) mobile application. Established in 1954, SP is Singapore's first polytechnic. Its ten academic schools offer 50 full-time diploma courses for its 16,000 students. Among SP's 160,000 graduates are successful entrepreneurs, top executives in multinational and public-listed corporations, and well-known professionals.
